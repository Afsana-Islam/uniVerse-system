What programs does BRAC University offer?

BRAC University offers undergraduate and graduate programs in fields such as Computer Science, Business Administration, Pharmacy, English, Law, Economics, Architecture, Electrical & Electronic Engineering, Biotechnology, and many more.

Added Aug 19, 2025

Are there scholarships or financial aid options?

Yes. BRAC University offers merit-based scholarships, financial aid, and need-based support for eligible students. Scholarships are awarded based on academic performance and other criteria.

Added Aug 19, 2025

What is the tuition fee structure?

Tuition fees vary by program. Undergraduate programs typically have per-credit tuition fees, along with semester charges. The latest fee structure is available on the official BRAC University website.

Added Aug 19, 2025

Does BRAC University accept international students?

Yes. BRAC University welcomes international students. International applicants need to submit their academic transcripts, English proficiency test scores (IELTS/TOEFL or equivalent), passport copy, and meet equivalency requirements.

Added Aug 19, 2025

How can I apply to BRAC University?

You can apply online through the official BRAC University admission portal. After submitting the form and paying the application fee, you will be notified about your admission test schedule.

Added Aug 19, 2025

What are the admission requirements?

The admission requirements include completing your application online, submitting transcripts from SSC/HSC or equivalent (O & A levels, IB, etc.), a recent photograph, and sitting for the admission test. English proficiency is also required.
